Honeymoon is a handwritten script typeface. 
It stands for confidence, effectivity and honesty. 
The letters' sharp and thin ink strokes cuts through the sentences like a razor. 

The font comes in both Regular and Bold, and includes an Upright version. 
It has multiple stylistic and contextual alternates, such as connecting pairs, 
final and initial alternates to make the lettering look as natural as possible.
